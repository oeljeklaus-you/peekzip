Privacy Policy for PeekZip

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

PeekZip is a local-first macOS archive preview and extraction app. This Privacy Policy explains how PeekZip handles information.

Information We Collect

PeekZip does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Local File Access

PeekZip may access archive files and selected output folders only when you choose them. File processing happens locally on your device. Your archive contents are not uploaded to our servers.

In-App Purchases

PeekZip Pro purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Analytics and Tracking

PeekZip does not use third-party advertising trackers. PeekZip does not track you across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data.

Children's Privacy

PeekZip is not designed to collect information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
